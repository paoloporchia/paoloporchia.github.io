Curriculum Vitae

Paolo Porchia is currently Adjunct Professor and a Fellow of the CASMEF Institute at the LUISS University in Rome, and a project collaborator at the University of Rome III. Previously, he has been Assistant Professor of Finance at the IE Business School in Madrid (2010-17), at the University of St. Gallen (2006-10), and a Post-Doctoral fellow in the latter institution (2005-2006). He holds a PhD in Finance from the University of Lugano (USI).

Field of Specialization

Asset Pricing, Empirical Asset Pricing, Asset Allocation, International Finance
